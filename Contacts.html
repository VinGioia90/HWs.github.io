Research Unit

The research unit is composed by Francesco Pauli, Gioia Di Credico, and Vincenzo Gioia. We are affiliated at the Department of Economics, Business, Mathematics and Statistics ``Bruno De Finetti" of the University of Trieste, Italy

Gioia Di Credico

Gioia Di Credico is Assistant Professor (tenure-track) at the University of Trieste (2025–present) and she holds a PhD in Statistics from the University of Padua. Her research focuses on Bayesian inference, semi-parametric modelling and computational statistics, with applications to environmental and health-related outcomes. She has published in leading journals including British Journal of Cancer, Journal of Computational and Graphical Statistics, Environmental and Ecological Statistics, and International Journal of Forecasting.

Vincenzo Gioia

Vincenzo Gioia is a post-doc researcher in Statistics from 2023. He received the PhD in Managerial and Actuarial Sciences, curriculum in Statistics and Actuarial Sciences, from the University of Udine and the University of Trieste in 2023. He has been visiting the School of Mathematics, University of Bristol, UK, supervised by prof. Matteo Fasiolo. His research focuses mainly on semi-parametric regression modelling techniques and multivariate modelling, applied to environmental, life sciences, and engineering contexts. He has published in Journal of the American Statistical Association, and alonng with Gioia Di Credico and Francesco Pauli, in Environmental and Ecological Statistics, and International Journal of Forecasting.

Francesco Pauli

Francesco Pauli is Full Professor of Statistics at the University of Trieste (since 2020) and Coordinator of the PhD programme in Applied Data Science and Artificial Intelligence. He earned his PhD in Statistics from the University of Padua. His research interests include methodological and computational statistics, Bayesian inference, extreme value theory, and semi-parametric modelling, with applications to environmental, ecological, and ageing-related issues. He is author of several publications including several in top-ranked journals such as Biometrika, Journal of the Royal Statistical Society, International statistical review , Environmetrics.
